Similar in design and function to the [[Trailing Jettison]], this device serves the Low City and parts of the Middle City. It is operated by a small band of otyughs who scour the city for rubbish to bring to the jettison and root through. Occasionally their street sweeping removes a few drunkards and beggars as well, but nobody complains. A clever, sarcastic neo-otyugh leads the band and directs their actions.
