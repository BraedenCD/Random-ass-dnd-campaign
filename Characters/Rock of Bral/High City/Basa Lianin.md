Basa Lianin of [[Locations/Rock of Bral/High City/House Lianin|House Lianin]] is an exceptional businesswoman and merchant. She prefers to deal in goods with high value density. She is a proud supporter of the Low City, constantly funding efforts to reduce crime and improve infrastructure. She often hires groups of adventurers to take down local crime lords. However... her true motives remain profit. She has found that the most effective method of attaining wealth is through the trade of illicit substances. Her goal by taking out local gangs is to eliminate competition. And yet, her contributions to the stability of the Low City are undeniable. She is a young woman who is often seen as abrasive due to her sheer enthusiasm.
