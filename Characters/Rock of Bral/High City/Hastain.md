Hastain is a reigar and a patron of the arts. He seems to have inexhaustible wealth, which he uses to fund artists of all disciplines and less widely-acceptable subject matters. He has an eye for the unexpected and outrageous and an appreciation for those who mind their own business. Despite his hordes of fans, he prefers to spend much of his time alone in [[Hastain's Palace|his palace]] and is quickly drained by social interaction. He often appears in public in formal capacities, but always seems slightly too tired. He has no known relatives.
