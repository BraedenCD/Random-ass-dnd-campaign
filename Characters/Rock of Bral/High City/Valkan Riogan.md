A fallen paladin of great renown, Valkan Riogan is one of the more unique faces among the nobles of Bral. He left Faerun and took to Wildspace after the love of his life was murdered, forsaking his oath in the process. He meandered for years until he came upon the Rock, where he found [[Prince Andru]] and swore a new oath, to protect Bral at all costs. He doesn't often speak about his past. He is the proud owner of the mercenary company [[Valkan's Legion]], home to the most prestigious mercs on the Rock. They are completely loyal to Valkan in every aspect, and strive to do good as often as possible... though a little extra coin never hurt anyone. He is among the most selfless of the nobles on Bral. He is in his late 30s and has a very well-worn appearance.
