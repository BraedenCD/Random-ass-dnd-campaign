Lord Ostric is the [[Royal Palace]]'s caretaker, overseeing the entirety of the palace grounds on behalf of [[Prince Andru|the prince]]. His years have taken a toll on him, but his work ethic remains unmatched. The prince has encouraged him to retire and spend time with his family many times over the years, be he has declined every time. He says that his greatest joy in life is caring for the Palace. He has earned the respect of practically everyone in the High City. He is quite tall, and his years show plainly. His voice can sound quite commanding when it needs to be.
